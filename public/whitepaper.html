2. The Problem with GameFi

Why 95% of GameFi Projects Fail

Problem 1: Extractive Tokenomics

  • High inflation (50-100%+ annually)
  • No sustainable sinks
  • "Play to earn" becomes "play to dump"

Problem 2: Unfair Distribution

  • VCs get 20-40% at massive discounts
  • Team gets 15-25% with short locks
  • Community gets dumped on at launch

Problem 3: No Real Utility

  • Token is just "governance" or staking
  • No actual burn mechanisms
  • Game is secondary to speculation

Problem 4: Unsustainable Yields

  • Promise 1,000%+ APY
  • Funded by new player money (Ponzi-like)
  • Collapses when new players stop
